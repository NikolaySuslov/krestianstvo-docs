The **4th version of Krestianstvo SDK** is distributed as a standalone [ESM module](https://www.npmjs.com/package/krestianstvo) for JavaScript. It could be easily bundled with any [Solid JS](https://www.solidjs.com/) or [Astro](https://astro.build/) web application being hosted and running just in the Cloud.

* [CodeSandbox demo project](https://codesandbox.io/s/krestianstvo-helloworld-pnimfu)
* Glitch demo bundled project with Reflector: https://krestianstvo-playground.glitch.me
* [Vercel](https://krestianstvo-playground.vercel.app/) Playground mirror
